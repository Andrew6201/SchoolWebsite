DIRECT APPLICATION

DIRECT APPLICATION IS TARGETED TO THOSE

  • who want to secure the study place at early stage and, e.g., allow plenty of time to organize the residence permit issues and housing
  • who are ready to confirm the study place within 7 days and pay the tuition fee within 14 days of being accepted as student

TIME TABLE AND APPLICATION PROCESS IN THE DIRECT APPLICATION 2024

  • Direct Application period opens on 1 November 2023.
  • Application period to each degree programme ends once the group is full. 
  • The applicant can apply only to one (1) degree programme in the application form.
  • Required attachments must be uploaded to the application form before the application form can be sent:
    • Copies of final Certificates
    • copies of official translations of your certificates, if they are not in English, Finnish or Swedish
    • a copy of the official passport / ID-card
  • Student selection is based on personal interview, in which the applicant’s motivation and the English language skills are evaluated.
  • After each week, Admission Services will check applications which have arrived. An applicant is eligible to the next step of the process, the personal interview, if she/he
    • has the eligibility to apply in the Direct application
    • has uploaded a copy of an official ID/passport on the application form
  • The results of the student selection will be published continuously, whenever the interviews have been held and the results are ready.
  • Admitted students must confirm their study place within 7 days of being accepted as student.
  • If the student is obligated to pay the tuition fee, the deadline for paying it is within 14 days of being accepted as student.
  • Studies will begin in the end of August 2024.
