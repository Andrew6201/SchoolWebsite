SCHOLARSHIP

Savonia UAS will not grant scholarships for the 1st academic year.

In the 2nd and upcoming academic years, the scholarship covers max. 50% of the tuition fee. To qualify for the scholarship of 40% of the tuition fee, the student must complete at least 55 credits during the previous academic year. The additional 10% of the tuition fee is based on the student’s level of proficiency in the Finnish language.

Savonia UAS grants scholarships only for the tuition fee. The scholarship is not paid to the student but is deducted directly from the tuition fee.

SCHOLARSHIP IN NUTSHELL

1st academic year
  • No scholarship
  • Tuition fee to be paid: 4800 €

2nd academic year

  • Scholarship: 40%
    • Requirement: 55 credits done in the previous academic year according to the curriculum
    • Tuition fee to be paid: 4800 €
  • + additional scholarship 10%
    • Requirement: if you have 55 credits and level of proficiency in the Finnish language A2.1
    • Tuition fee to be paid: 4000 €
  • Tuition fee to be paid if you do not fulfill the scholarship criteria by 31 July: 8000€

3rd academic year

  • Scholarship: 40%
    • Requirement: 55 credits done in the previous academic year according to the curriculum
    • Tuition fee to be paid: 4800 €
  • + additional scholarship 10%
    • Requirement: if you have 55 credits and level of proficiency in the Finnish language B1.1
    • Tuition fee to be paid: 4000 €
  • Tuition fee to be paid if you do not fulfill the scholarship criteria by 31 July: 8000€

4th academic year

  • Scholarship: 40%
    • Requirement: 55 credits done in the previous academic year according to the curriculum
    • Tuition fee to be paid: 4800 €
  • + additional scholarship 10%
    • Requirement: if you have 55 credits and level of proficiency in the Finnish language B1.1
    • Tuition fee to be paid: 4000 €
  • Tuition fee to be paid if you do not fulfill the scholarship criteria by 31 July: 8000€

NEXT STUDY YEARS

The scholarship can only be granted for the statutory study time. If you continue your studies after the statutory study time, the tuition fee to be paid is 8000 € per academic year.

Statutory study times for different extents of study programmes are:

  • 210 credits Bachelor’s degree – 3,5 years
  • 240 credits Bachelor’s degree – 4 years

DEVELOPING FINNISH LANGUAGE SKILLS

Scholarship based on humanitarian reasons for Ukrainians

As an exception to normal scholarship scheme, Savonia UAS grants scholarships to Ukrainian degree students in Finland. These students will get a full scholarship based on humanitarian reasons and need for protection, so that they do not need to pay the tuition fees. The scholarship is granted based on a valid passport or other ID, which indicates, that the student is a citizen of Ukraine.
