JOINT APPLICATION

JOINT APPLICATION IS TARGETED TO THOSE

  • who want to apply also to other UASs or degree programmes with the same application form
  • who want to use the joint student selection method, e.g., the International UAS Exam or SAT test.

TIME TABLE FOR THE JOINT APPLICATION 2024

  • Joint Application period is from 3 to 17 January 2024.
  • Application form must be submitted in StudyInfo.fi 17 January 2024 at 3:00 p.m. Finnish time
  • Required attachments must be uploaded to the application form by 24 January 2024 3:00 p.m. Finnish time.
    • if you have already graduated, copies of final certificates
    • if you will graduate after the application period, copy of your latest transcript or other statement from your school
    • copies of official translations of your certificates/transcripts if they are not in English, Finnish or Swedish
    • a copy of the official passport / ID-card
  • Student selection can be done in two ways:
    • International UAS Exam
      • The applicant must have a biometric passport
      • The applicant must have a proof of English language skills
    • SAT test score
      • The applicant must have a minimum of 500 points of the section “Evidence-Based Reading and Writing”
      • The applicant must have a minimum of 500 points of the section “Mathematics”
  • Results of the student selection will be published 31 May 2024.
  • Admitted students must confirm their study place by 11 July 2024 at the latest, or if accepted from the waiting list in June or July, within 7 days of being accepted as a student.
  • The waiting list is valid until 30 July 2024 3:00 p.m. Finnish time.
  • Those who are obligated to pay the tuition fee will receive instructions on how to pay the tuition fee.
  • Studies will begin in the end of August 2024.
